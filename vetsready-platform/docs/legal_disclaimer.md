## When to Consult a Professional

### For VA Claims & Benefits:
- Consult a **VA-accredited representative** (free from VSO)
- Consult a **VA disability attorney** (for complex claims)
- Contact your **Veterans Service Organization (VSO)**

### For Medical Concerns:
- Consult your **primary care physician**
- Contact **VA Healthcare**
- Call the **Veterans Crisis Line**: 988 then press 1
- Visit **Veterans.gov** for resources

### For Legal Matters:
- Consult a **licensed attorney**
- Contact **Legal Aid** services in your state
- Reach out to **USERRA** specialists for workplace rights
- Contact the **ADA** regional office for disability rights

### For Financial Planning:
- Consult a **Certified Financial Planner (CFP)**
- Contact **NFCC** for credit counseling
- Consult a **CPA** for tax matters

### For Business Matters:
- Consult a **business attorney**
- Contact the **Small Business Administration (SBA)**
- Work with a **business mentor**
