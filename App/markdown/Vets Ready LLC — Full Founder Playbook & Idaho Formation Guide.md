1. Idaho LLC Formation Steps
1.1 Choose the Business Structure
* Vets Ready LLC will operate as a Limited Liability Company in Idaho.
* LLC chosen for liability protection, tax flexibility, and simplicity.
1.2 Create an SOSBiz Account
* Visit the Idaho Secretary of State SOSBiz portal.
* Create an account to manage filings, annual reports, and business records.
1.3 File the Certificate of Organization
* File online through SOSBiz.
* Required information:
* LLC name: Vets Ready LLC
* Business address: 1895 W. Crenshaw St., Kuna, Idaho 83634
* Registered agent name and Idaho address
* Organizer information
* Pay the state filing fee.
1.4 Appoint a Registered Agent
* Must have a physical Idaho address.
* Can be the owner or a commercial registered agent.
1.5 Obtain an EIN (Employer Identification Number)
* Apply for free at IRS.gov.
* Required for banking, taxes, and hiring.
1.6 Register for Idaho State Taxes
* Register through the Idaho Business Registration System.
* Required if Vets Ready LLC will:
* Sell taxable goods or services
* Hire employees
* Withhold sales or income tax
1.7 Obtain Required Licenses and Permits
* Idaho does not require a general business license statewide.
* Check Ada County and City of Kuna for local requirements.
* No special licensing required for software/platform businesses.
1.8 Create an Operating Agreement
* Not required by Idaho law but strongly recommended.
* Defines ownership, management, and operating rules.
1.9 Open a Business Bank Account
* Bring EIN, Certificate of Organization, and Operating Agreement.
* Keep finances separate for liability protection.
1.10 File Annual Reports
* Idaho requires annual reports for LLCs.
* Must be filed through SOSBiz each year.
