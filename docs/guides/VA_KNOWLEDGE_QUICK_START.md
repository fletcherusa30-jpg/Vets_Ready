## Using AI Search

### Step 1: Switch to AI Search Tab
Click **AI Search** button in the hero section

### Step 2: Ask a Question
**Type your question** or **click a suggested question:**
- "How is VA disability rating calculated?"
- "What is service connection and how do I prove it?"
- "What are common secondary conditions?"
- "How do I file a PTSD claim?"
- "What evidence do I need for my claim?"
- "How do I appeal a denied claim?"

### Step 3: Get AI Response
- Click **Ask AI** or press **Enter**
- Wait 2 seconds for AI processing
- Review comprehensive answer with sources

### Step 4: Ask Follow-up Questions
Continue asking questions to build your knowledge
