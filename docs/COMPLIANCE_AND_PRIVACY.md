## Overview

VetsReady is committed to providing compliant, ethical, and veteran-centric assistance for VA disability claims. This document outlines our legal boundaries, data handling practices, and privacy commitments.

### Core Principles

1. **Educational Only** - All content and AI-generated output is educational, not legal or medical advice
2. **Veteran Privacy** - Veterans control their data; we never access VA systems without permission
3. **VA Respect** - We do not scrape, automate, or interfere with VA systems
4. **Transparency** - Clear disclaimers on every page and AI interaction
5. **User Empowerment** - Veterans own their data and can delete it anytime

---

## Legal Boundaries

### ✅ What VetsReady DOES

- **Provides educational guidance** on the VA disability claims process
- **Generates AI-powered theories** of entitlement labeled as "educational only"
- **Offers tools** for organizing evidence, tracking claims, and calculating benefits
- **Suggests secondary conditions** based on medical literature and VA patterns
- **Calculates effective dates** using publicly available AMA regulations
- **Provides financial planning tools** using official VA rate tables
- **Connects veterans** with accredited VSOs and VA-accredited attorneys
- **Shares educational content** about 38 CFR, M21-1, and VA processes

### ❌ What VetsReady DOES NOT DO

- **NO VA.gov/eBenefits scraping** - We never scrape, crawl, or automated extract data from VA websites
- **NO credential storage** - We never store VA usernames, passwords, or login credentials
- **NO automated VA interactions** - We do not automate logins, submissions, or interactions with VA systems
- **NO legal advice** - We do not provide legal advice or create attorney-client relationships
- **NO medical diagnoses** - AI suggestions are educational; only licensed physicians can diagnose
- **NO guarantees** - We do not guarantee claim approval or specific outcomes
- **NO unauthorized representation** - Only accredited VSOs and VA-accredited attorneys can represent veterans

### Specific Prohibited Activities

1. **Web Scraping**: No automated scraping of VA.gov, eBenefits, or any VA system
2. **Credential Harvesting**: No collection, storage, or use of VA login credentials
3. **Automated Filing**: No automated submission of VA claims or forms
4. **Impersonation**: No representation as VA employees or official VA agents
5. **Guaranteed Outcomes**: No promises of specific ratings or claim approvals
6. **Unlicensed Practice**: No legal advice without proper accreditation
7. **Medical Diagnosis**: No diagnosis of conditions (only educational suggestions)

## Data Handling

### What Data We Collect

#### Local Storage (No Account)
- **Wizard State**: Disabilities, conditions, AI theories, evidence notes
- **Claim Tracker**: Claim statuses, dates, notes
- **Preferences**: Theme, settings
- **Storage Location**: Browser localStorage (on user's device)
- **Retention**: Until user clears browser data

#### Backend Storage (With Account - Optional)
- **Account Information**: Email, password (hashed), profile data
- **Claim Data**: Synced wizard state, conditions, theories
- **Evidence Metadata**: File names, types, tags, notes (files stored separately)
- **Usage Metrics**: Page views, feature usage (anonymized)
- **Storage Location**: PostgreSQL database (encrypted at rest)
- **Retention**: Until user deletes account

### What Data We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ VA usernames or passwords
- ❌ Social Security Numbers (SSNs)
- ❌ VA file numbers
- ❌ Actual medical records (we store metadata only, not documents)
- ❌ Browsing history outside VetsReady
- ❌ Location data (except for VSO locator with consent)

### Data Categories & Classification

| Data Type | Classification | Storage | Encryption |
|-----------|---------------|---------|------------|
| Email | PII | Database | At rest |
| Password | Sensitive | Database | Bcrypt hash |
| Name | PII | Database | At rest |
| Service Dates | Personal | Database | At rest |
| Disability Names | Medical | Database/Local | At rest |
| Ratings | Medical | Database/Local | At rest |
| Evidence Files | Medical | S3 (optional) | At rest + transit |
| Usage Metrics | Anonymized | Database | At rest |

### Data Minimization

We follow the principle of **data minimization**:
- Collect only what's necessary for the feature
- Use anonymized/pseudonymized data when possible
- Allow local-only operation (no account required)
- Automatically delete unused data after 2 years of inactivity

## Security Practices

### Authentication & Authorization

- **Password Hashing**: Bcrypt with salt (cost factor 12)
- **JWT Tokens**: Short-lived (1 hour), refresh tokens (7 days)
- **Session Management**: Secure, HttpOnly cookies
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**: Optional (coming in Phase 3)

### Data Encryption

- **In Transit**: TLS 1.3 for all HTTPS connections
- **At Rest**: AES-256 encryption for database (AWS RDS)
- **File Storage**: Server-side encryption (S3 SSE-KMS)
- **Backups**: Encrypted backups with separate keys

### API Security

- **Rate Limiting**: 100 requests/minute per IP
- **Input Validation**: Pydantic schemas for all endpoints
- **SQL Injection Prevention**: Parameterized queries (SQLAlchemy ORM)
- **XSS Prevention**: React sanitizes output by default
- **CSRF Protection**: CSRF tokens for state-changing operations

### Logging & Monitoring

- **PII Sanitization**: Remove names, SSNs, emails from logs
- **Error Logging**: Sentry for error tracking (no PII)
- **Access Logs**: CloudWatch (IP anonymization after 30 days)
- **Audit Trail**: Account actions logged (login, delete, export)

### Incident Response

- **Breach Notification**: 72 hours to affected users (GDPR compliance)
- **Security Updates**: Automatic dependency updates (Dependabot)
- **Penetration Testing**: Annual third-party security audits
- **Bug Bounty**: Responsible disclosure program (coming Phase 4)

## Privacy Principles

### 1. Local-First Architecture

- **Default**: All data stored in browser localStorage
- **Optional Sync**: Users can create accounts to sync across devices
- **No Tracking**: No Google Analytics or invasive tracking (use Plausible)

### 2. Transparency

- **Clear Policies**: Privacy policy and terms of service in plain language
- **Data Usage**: Explain how data is used on collection forms
- **AI Transparency**: Label all AI-generated content

### 3. User Control

- **Granular Permissions**: Users choose what to sync
- **Data Export**: Download all data as JSON (GDPR compliance)
- **Right to Delete**: One-click account and data deletion
- **Opt-Out**: Users can opt out of analytics and optional features

### 4. Purpose Limitation

- **Use Only for Stated Purpose**: Data used only for providing VetsReady features
- **No Third-Party Sales**: We never sell user data
- **Minimal Sharing**: Share only with service providers (OpenAI, AWS) as needed

### 5. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Email reminder after 1 year, deletion after 2 years
- **Deleted Accounts**: Immediate deletion from production database
- **Backups**: Removed from backups within 30 days

## User Rights

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)

1. **Right to Access**: Download all your data
2. **Right to Rectification**: Update incorrect data
3. **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**: Delete your account and data
4. **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
5. **Right to Data Portability**: Export data in machine-readable format (JSON)
6. **Right to Object**: Opt out of analytics and non-essential processing
7. **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Revoke permissions anytime

### CCPA Rights (California Users)

1. **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect
2. **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of personal information
3. **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
4. **Right to Non-Discrimination**: No discrimination for exercising rights

### How to Exercise Rights

- **Account Settings**: Edit profile, export data, delete account
- **Email Request**: privacy@vetsready.com (respond within 30 days)
- **Automated Tools**: Self-service data export and deletion

## Regulatory Compliance

### HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)

**Status**: VetsReady is **NOT** a HIPAA-covered entity because:
- We are not a healthcare provider, health plan, or healthcare clearinghouse
- We do not create, receive, or transmit PHI (Protected Health Information) on behalf of covered entities
- Veterans voluntarily enter their own data (not transmitted from healthcare providers)

**However**, we follow HIPAA-inspired best practices:
- Encrypt medical data at rest and in transit
- Limit access to medical data (role-based access control)
- Audit logs for data access
- Secure disposal of data upon deletion

### FTC (Federal Trade Commission) Guidelines

- **Truth in Advertising**: No false claims about VA approval rates or guaranteed outcomes
- **Endorsements**: Clearly label any testimonials as individual experiences
- **Privacy**: Follow FTC privacy guidelines for data handling
- **Security**: Reasonable security measures to protect user data

### ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) - Web Accessibility

- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**: Minimum standard
- **Screen Reader Support**: ARIA labels and semantic HTML
- **Keyboard Navigation**: All features accessible without mouse
- **Alternative Formats**: Transcripts for video/audio content
