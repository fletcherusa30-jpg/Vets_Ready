## üîç LEAD QUALIFICATION CRITERIA

### What Makes a "Qualified Lead"?

**Veteran Must:**
1. ‚úÖ Have completed claims analysis on our platform
2. ‚úÖ Be VA benefits-eligible (verified service)
3. ‚úÖ Have legal representation need (appeal, complex case, etc.)
4. ‚úÖ Opted in to attorney matching
5. ‚úÖ Provided verified contact information
6. ‚úÖ Meet minimum case value threshold ($5,000+ potential benefit increase)

**Case Types We Generate:**

**High-Value Cases:**
- **VA Appeals** (BVA, Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims)
- **TDIU (Total Disability Individual Unemployability)**
- **SMC (Special Monthly Compensation)** - housebound, aid & attendance
- **Multiple secondary conditions** (complex nexus arguments)
- **Discharge upgrades** (military records correction)
- **Denied claims with new evidence**

**Medium-Value Cases:**
- Rating increase appeals (70% ‚Üí 100%)
- Secondary condition denials
- Effective date disputes
- DIC (Dependency and Indemnity Compensation) claims
- Burn pit exposure claims
- Gulf War syndrome cases

**What We Don't Send:**
- First-time claims (can be filed pro se)
- Simple increase requests (< 30% to 50%)
- Cases better suited for VSO representation
- Non-viable cases (no legal merit)
