Vets Ready ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

**By using Vets Ready, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.**

**Military Service Information:**

**Health and Medical Information:**
- Medical conditions you disclose

**VA Claims Information:**

- Pending claims

- Evidence and documentation

**Financial Information:**
- Payment method (processed by Stripe)
- Billing address
- Subscription history

**Communications:**
- Support requests
- Feedback and surveys
- Messages with VSO representatives

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**
- Pages visited
- Features used
- Time spent on Service
- Click patterns
- Search queries

**Device Information:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Screen resolution

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies:**
- Session cookies (required for login)
- Analytics cookies (optional)
- Preference cookies (optional)

**OAuth Authentication:**

**VSO Partners:**

**Payment Processors:**

**Encryption:**
- TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit

- Encrypted database backups

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication for staff
- Regular access audits

**Infrastructure Security:**
- Firewalls and intrusion detection
- Regular security updates
- Vulnerability scanning
- Penetration testing

**Monitoring:**
- Real-time security alerts
- Audit logging
- Suspicious activity detection

### 4.2 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:
- Notify affected users within 72 hours
- Provide details about the breach
- Explain steps we're taking to address it
- Offer guidance on protecting your account

### 4.3 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:
- Choosing a strong, unique password
- Keeping your login credentials confidential
- Logging out of shared devices
- Reporting suspicious activity

## 6. Special Protections for Sensitive Data

### 6.1 Health Information (HIPAA)

While we are not a covered entity under HIPAA, we treat health information with HIPAA-level protections:
- Minimum necessary access
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Audit logging of all access
- Business Associate Agreements with vendors

### 6.2 Military Records

We handle military records with special care:
- DD-214s are encrypted
- Access is logged and monitored
- Data is stored in U.S.-based facilities
- Compliance with DoD data standards

### 6.3 Financial Information

We do NOT store full credit card numbers or CVV codes. Stripe handles all payment processing.
