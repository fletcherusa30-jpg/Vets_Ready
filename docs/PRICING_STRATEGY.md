## ðŸŽ¯ VETERAN VALUE PROPOSITION

**"We charge companies to help you, not charge you for help."**

### What Veterans Get:
1. **Nearly free access** ($20/year = 2 cups of coffee)
2. **No upsells or paywalls** on core features
3. **Privacy protected** - we never sell personal data
4. **Quality partners only** - vetted employers and businesses
5. **Transparent referrals** - always disclosed when we earn commission
6. **Mission-driven company** - veteran success is the metric

### What Makes This Work:
1. **Employers want veteran talent** (they'll pay premium)
2. **Businesses want veteran customers** (high loyalty, spending power)
3. **Scale matters** - 1M veterans @ $20/yr = $20M, but real money is B2B
4. **Trust builds community** - veterans tell other veterans
5. **Data is valuable** - anonymized market intelligence worth millions
