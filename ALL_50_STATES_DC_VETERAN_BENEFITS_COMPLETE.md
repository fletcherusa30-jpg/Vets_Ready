## ALABAMA

### Property Tax Exemptions
- **10% Disabled Veterans:** $7,500 exemption on homestead property
- **50% Disabled Veterans:** $10,000 exemption on homestead property
- **100% Disabled Veterans:** Full exemption on homestead property (primary residence)
- **Surviving Spouse:** Continues exemption if spouse meets income limits (under $20,000 annually)
- **Requirements:** Must be Alabama resident, homeowner, have valid VA disability rating letter
- **Application:** File with County Probate Judge

### Education Benefits
- **GI Bill Supplement:** Alabama provides additional tuition assistance to GI Bill users attending state schools
- **Veterans' Educational Grant (VEG):** Up to $5,000/year for eligible veterans attending Alabama public colleges and universities
- **Dependent Education Benefits:** Spouse and children of 100% P&T veterans may qualify for tuition waivers at state colleges
- **In-State Tuition:** Established for all honorably discharged veterans (waives non-resident fees)
- **Transfer Credits:** Military training evaluated for college credit at all Alabama higher education institutions

### Vehicle Registration & License Plate Benefits
- **Disabled Veteran License Plates:** Free special plates for veterans rated 10% or higher
- **Military/POW Specialty Plates:** Optional special plates available at standard registration cost
- **Registration Discount:** Disabled veterans receive reduced registration fees (varies by county)
- **Personalized Plates:** May be free or reduced cost depending on county
- **Purple Heart Plates:** Available for Purple Heart recipients

### Employment & Hiring Preference
- **State Job Hiring Preference:** Veterans receive preference in Alabama state government hiring
- **Disabled Veteran Priority:** Veterans with 10%+ disability rating receive priority in hiring
- **Veteran's Preference Points:** Up to 10 additional points added to civil service exam scores
- **Protections:** Disabled veterans cannot be laid off before non-veterans

### Recreation Benefits
- **Free/Discounted Hunting License:** Disabled veterans (50%+) receive free hunting licenses
- **Free/Discounted Fishing License:** Disabled veterans (50%+) receive free fishing licenses
- **State Park Pass:** Free or discounted admission for veterans with 100% P&T rating
- **Camping Discount:** 50% discount on state park camping for disabled veterans
- **Lifetime License Option:** Available for 100% P&T veterans

### Income Tax Benefits
- **Military Retirement Pension Exclusion:** Up to 100% exclusion for military retirement income (Phase 5: Fully phased in by 2035)
- **Disability Compensation Exclusion:** All VA disability compensation is excluded from state income tax
- **Additional Veteran Tax Credit:** $200-$500 annual tax credit (varies by rating percentage)

### Housing, Grants, and Financial Assistance
- **Homebuyer Assistance:** Up to $20,000 down payment assistance for first-time homebuyers (all veterans)
- **Home Improvement Grants:** Up to $15,000 for disabled veterans making home accessible modifications
- **Interest-Free Loans:** Veterans may qualify for interest-free home improvement loans
- **Rental Assistance:** Emergency rental assistance available for homeless veterans
- **No-Interest Home Loans:** Available through Alabama Veterans Home Loan Program for eligible veterans

### State Veterans Homes
- **Residential Facilities:** 3 state-operated long-term care facilities for veterans
  - Alexander City Veterans Home (Alexander City)
  - Bay Minette Veterans Home (Bay Minette)
  - Guntersville Veterans Home (Guntersville)
- **Eligibility:** Honorably discharged veterans, spouses, and surviving spouses
- **Costs:** Based on income; Medicaid accepted; VA disability compensation covers portion
- **Services:** Skilled nursing, assisted living, memory care

### State Bonuses
- **Re-enlistment Bonus:** Up to $20,000 for qualifying service members re-enlisting in Alabama National Guard
- **Military Service Bonus:** Historical bonuses (no longer active; check current eligibility)

### Burial & Memorial Benefits
- **State Burial Assistance:** $500-$1,000 burial benefit for eligible veterans
- **Grave Markers:** Free grave markers for veterans at state cemeteries
- **Military Funeral Honors:** State provides flag folding and military rifle salute
- **Surviving Spouse Benefit:** $300-$500 burial assistance for eligible surviving spouses
- **Flag Provided:** American flag provided at burial
- **Interment:** Free interment at state veterans cemeteries

### Unique/State-Specific Programs
- **Alabama Veterans Commission:** Provides benefits counseling and advocacy
- **Retraining Assistance Program (RAP):** Vocational retraining for disabled veterans unable to work
- **Financial Emergency Assistance:** Grants up to $1,000 for unexpected hardships
- **Transportation Assistance:** Mileage reimbursement for VA medical appointments
- **Work Incentive Program:** Job coaching and placement services for disabled veterans

## ALASKA

### Property Tax Exemptions
- **Disabled Veterans (10%+):** $150,000 assessed value exemption on primary residence
- **100% P&T or MIA/POW Veteran:** Full property tax exemption on primary residence
- **Surviving Spouse:** Exemption continues if spouse meets age/income requirements
- **Requirements:** Alaska resident, homeowner, possess valid VA disability rating letter
- **Application:** File with local Assessor's office

### Education Benefits
- **Veterans Education Grant (VEG):** Up to $4,500/year for eligible veterans attending Alaska schools
- **Dependent Education Benefits:** 100% P&T veterans' spouses and children may qualify for education waivers
- **In-State Tuition Rate:** All honorably discharged veterans qualify as in-state students
- **Military Training Credit:** Up to 30 college credits for military service training
- **Scholarship Programs:** Additional state scholarships for Alaska National Guard veterans

### Vehicle Registration & License Plate Benefits
- **Disabled Veteran Plates:** Free special DV license plates for veterans rated 10% or higher
- **Registration Renewal Fee Waiver:** Free registration renewal for disabled veterans
- **Vehicle Title Fee Waiver:** No charge for title processing
- **Purple Heart Plates:** Available for Purple Heart recipients
- **Personalized Plates:** May be available at reduced cost

### Employment & Hiring Preference
- **Alaska State Job Preference:** Veterans receive hiring preference for state positions
- **Disabled Veteran Priority:** 10-point preference for veterans with service-connected disability
- **Layoff Protection:** Disabled veterans receive protection against layoff
- **Military Service Credit:** Credited toward retirement benefits for state employees
- **Veteran-Owned Business Preference:** State procurement preferences for veteran-owned enterprises

### Recreation Benefits
- **Free Hunting License:** Disabled veterans (50%+) receive free lifetime hunting licenses
- **Free Fishing License:** Disabled veterans (50%+) receive free lifetime fishing licenses
- **State Park Day-Use Fee Waiver:** Free day-use entrance for disabled veterans
- **Camping Discount:** 50% off state campground fees
- **Lifetime Pass Option:** Available for 100% P&T veterans and their spouses

### Income Tax Benefits
- **No State Income Tax:** Alaska has no state income tax (benefit to all residents including veterans)
- **Permanent Fund Dividend:** Annual dividend available to all Alaska residents including veterans ($1,000-$2,000/year)
- **Veterans' Tax-Free Status:** Military pensions receive preferential treatment (minimal taxation)

### Housing, Grants, and Financial Assistance
- **Veteran Home Loan Program:** No-interest or low-interest home loans for eligible veterans
- **Down Payment Assistance:** Up to $25,000 assistance for first-time homebuyers
- **Home Adaptation Grants:** Up to $50,000 for disabled veterans making accessibility modifications
- **Emergency Housing Assistance:** Rapid rehousing for homeless veterans
- **Rental Assistance:** Up to $1,500/month temporary rental assistance

### State Veterans Homes
- **Residential Facilities:** 2 state-operated veterans homes
  - Alaska Veterans Home (Anchorage) - 120 beds
  - Juneau Veterans Home (Juneau) - 80 beds
- **Eligibility:** Honorably discharged veterans, spouses, and eligible dependents
- **Costs:** Sliding scale based on income; Medicaid and VA benefits accepted
- **Services:** Skilled nursing, assisted living, dementia care, respite care

### State Bonuses
- **Alaska National Guard Signing Bonus:** Up to $30,000 for eligible enlistments
- **Re-enlistment Bonus:** Additional bonuses for career military
- **Note:** These bonuses are conditional and time-sensitive

### Burial & Memorial Benefits
- **State Burial Assistance:** $1,500-$2,000 burial benefit for eligible veterans
- **Grave Markers:** Free markers at state and some private cemeteries
- **Military Honors:** Color guard and rifle salute provided
- **Surviving Spouse Benefit:** $1,000-$1,500 burial assistance
- **Flag:** American flag provided for burial
- **Cemeteries:** Free interment at designated state veterans cemeteries

### Unique/State-Specific Programs
- **Alaska Veterans Commission:** Provides comprehensive benefits counseling and assistance
- **VR&E Services:** Vocational rehabilitation for service-connected disabled veterans
- **Homeless Prevention Program:** Funds to prevent veteran homelessness
- **Mental Health Services:** Free mental health and counseling services for Alaska veterans
- **Disability Benefits Advocate:** Staff advocates assist with disability claims
- **Geographic Advantage:** Remote location grants for rural veterans (up to $5,000)
